Temple Spotlight: Oquirrh Mountain Temple

The Oquirrh Mountain Temple, located in South Jordan, Utah in the United States, was dedicated August 2009 by Thomas S. Monson making it the 13th temple built in Utah and the fourth built in the Salt Lake Valley. The temple spire was truck by lightening on June 13, 2009, during the public open house. A replacement statue was installed on August 11, 2009, just 10 days prior to the dedicatory services. The Oquirrh Moutain Temple sits on a bluff in the Daybreak community. Within a few minutes walk from the temple, patrons have access to shopping, trails, and recreation around Daybreak Lake.
