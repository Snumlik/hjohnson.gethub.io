Site Name and Domain

Site Purpose

Keeping you updated on local Idaho weather!

This webpage is intended to describe the WebDevWeather.com website, it's purpose, intended audience, and other design aspects.

The WebDevWeather.com website will provide residence and visitors of the Idaho area current information on the weather forcasts and community events (banners). This will help plan for travel, preparation for local activities, and personal outdoor gatherings.
