Target Audience

Individuals interested in local Idaho weather and amateur web design. Children as young as seven take an interest in weather, our website will be designed with users young and old in mind.

Persona One: Autistic Child

  • Fictional Name: Gage Johnson
  • Demographics: Single child, 8 years old, attends 2nd grade at the local elementary school.
  • Goals and Tasks: Gage fixates on weather. He wants to know what the weather is like in multiple places around the world at any given time. He checks the weather a few times a day and keeps his family updated on the statistics.
  • Environment: Gage is a minor, uses old iphone or his amazon fire to check the weather. Doesn’t use a computer, but likes to be updated on the go.
  • Quote: “You know it’s 73 degrees Farenheit in my neighborhood but it’s 65 degrees Farinheit Idaho.”

Persona Two: Runner

  • Fictional Name: Lisa James
  • Demographics: 57 years old, active marathoner, Financial Advisor, Mother of 4, Grandmother of 5, Masters in Governmental Accounting.
  • Goals and Tasks:Focused, leadership positions, long time finance workers within different sections of local governments. Runs for stress relief and sanity. Needs to know the weather prior to runs to dress appropriately. Trying to run a marathon in every state. Loves to hike after runs.
  • Environment: iPhone owner but regularly has iPad on hand for the larger viewing screen. Comfortable with Mac and Windows workstations, uses computer for work, dial-up at home.
  • Quote: “You can always take off layers on a run, but adding layers is more difficult.”
