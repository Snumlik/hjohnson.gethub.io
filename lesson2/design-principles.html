Design Principles

White Space and Clean Design

Flats Luxury Suites

Flats Luxury Suites brand and webpage utilizes a modern use of clean design. Their website presents an air of sophistication by utilizing whitespace to their advantage.

Visual Hierarchy

BYU Idaho

BYU Idaho's home page is an excellent representation of Visual Hierarchy design. The listing of links and articles on thier homepage are arranged in a verticle list starting with the most searched for inquiry. Subsequent links are important but only for a smaller group of users.

Rule of Thirds

Squarespace

Squarespace, a web development company, strategially used a bold image as their homepage. The image has excellent photographic composition, utilizing nuetral tones making it applicable to most users, gantastic framing, and exemplifies the rule of thirds. Squarespace has further played on this rule of thids by framing a portion of the image within a mock-viewport. The image within this frame is also following the rule of thirds, and the frame within the user's screen is also following the rule of thirds.
